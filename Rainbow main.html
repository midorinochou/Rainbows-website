Welcome to the Rainbow world!


Ever wanted to have a rainbow for a warbrobe? Well, maybe not an actual rainbow, but wouldn't it be awesome if everything in your wardrobe was color-coded according to the colors of the rainbow? And not just that, if you actually owened clothes in the colors of the rainbow?

Rainbow World is the place for you!
This is how we work: you pick your starting color, for example green, then choose your category, let's say skirts, then either choose a particular style in that category, let's say A-line skirts or select "All" and hit the RAINBOWFY ME button. We will then do some magic in the background and present you with all A-line skirts in any shade of green that we have in stock!

Repeat for all colors!
